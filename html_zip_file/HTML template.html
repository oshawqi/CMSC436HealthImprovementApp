Weight Room is an application meant to make working out more social again. It combines the connectivity of social media with the competitive aspects of working out and staying fit. Users are able to view some of our favorite workouts immediately, create their own. Even better, if you find yourself bored of your own curriculums, you can browse every user's custom workouts to find new ideas and routines. Find workouts from people all over the world to see how you compare to your fellow exercise enthusiasts. Our app features simple yet elegant user interface that everyone should be able to use. Try it for yourself today and take your fitness program to the next level!
